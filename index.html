What we do

At Datarythm we help you incorporate drone technology in your business workflow. Our areas of expertise are...

  • Drone survey and mapping.
  • Photogrammetry and Computer Vision.
  • Geo-spatial analytics.

    • Services

    We provide a wide range of services.

    Drone Survey

    We excel in capturing the data for your needs. We have a fleet of drones and smart sensors best suited for your needs.

    Photogrammetry and Computer Vision

    Using state of the art Photogrammetry softwares we will process your data into standard geo-spatial formats, such as Orthomosaics, DEM, DSM and 3D maps.

    Geo-spatial Analytics

    Once the data is available in standard Geo-spatial formats, we use our expertise in analytics to find the most useful information in your data. We integrate modern machine learning techniques for best quality outputs.

    Consulting

    If you have a question or would like to know how drones can be used in your business, we are there to help you as consultants.
